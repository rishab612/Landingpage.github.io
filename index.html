Come and savor every bite with us, where culinary excellence meets heartfelt hospitality.

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a timeless classic in Indian cuisine. Tender chicken pieces are marinated in a blend of spices and yogurt, then cooked to perfection in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce. The dish is characterized by its vibrant color and luxurious texture, offering a perfect balance of savory spices and smooth, buttery flavor. Served with naan or basmati rice, our Butter Chicken promises an unforgettable culinary experience.

₹750.90

Hyderabadi Biryani

Hyderabadi Biryani is a legendary dish that hails from the royal kitchens of Hyderabad. This exquisite biryani is made with long-grain basmati rice, marinated meat (chicken, lamb, or goat), and a blend of aromatic spices. Slow-cooked to perfection, the layers of marinated meat and rice are infused with saffron and herbs, creating a rich, fragrant, and flavorful dish. Accompanied by a side of cooling raita, our Hyderabadi Biryani offers a taste of regal Indian cuisine that is both satisfying and memorable.

₹580.99

Kerala Chicken Curry

Kerala Chicken Curry is a delightful blend of flavors from the southwestern coast of India. This aromatic curry features tender chicken pieces simmered in a rich and creamy coconut milk base, infused with a medley of spices such as turmeric, coriander, and garam masala. The addition of curry leaves and mustard seeds gives it a distinctive taste, while the use of fresh tomatoes and onions adds depth and sweetness. Served with steamed rice or appam, our Kerala Chicken Curry offers a taste of the tropics that is both comforting and exotic.

₹550.98

Butter Paneer

Butter Paneer, also known as Paneer Makhani, is a delightful vegetarian dish that showcases the rich flavors of Indian cuisine. Soft, succulent cubes of paneer are simmered in a luscious, creamy tomato-based sauce infused with aromatic spices. The smooth, buttery gravy perfectly complements the paneer, creating a harmonious blend of textures and flavors. Served with naan or basmati rice, our Butter Paneer is a sumptuous and satisfying choice for any lover of fine vegetarian cuisine.

Rogan Josh

Rogan josh is a staple of Kashmiri cuisine. It consists of braised lamb chunks cooked with gravy made from browned onions, yogurt, garlic, ginger, and aromatic spices. Known for its brilliant red color, a classic rogan josh uses liberal amounts of dried Kashmiri chilis.

Vindaloo

Vindaloo is a flavorful curry dish, well known in Goa and Konkan. It is typically made with marinated pork, but it can also be made with chicken, beef, mutton, or paneer. The dish is named after the Portuguese dish, carne de vinha d’alhos, meaning meat marinated in wine vinegar and garlic. Many Indian spices are incorporated into the dish, such as tamarind, cinnamon, cardamom, and hot chilis.

Malabar Fish Curry

This authentic fish curry originates from Malabar, Kerala. The curry is typically made with seer, sea bass, tilapia, or sardines. Other ingredients include onions, tomatoes, hot chili peppers, chili powder, curry leaves, coriander, turmeric, garlic, fenugreek, tamarind, oil, and coconut milk, which lends the dish its rich creaminess. It is flavored with deep and earthy Indian spices such as fried curry leaves and tamarind.

Ras Malai

Ras Malai is a much-loved, delicious Indian dessert of West Bengali origin made from white cream, sugar, milk, and cardamom-flavored paneer cheese known as chhana. Cashews, almonds and saffron are often added. Spongy, soft, and aromatic, it is typically served chilled and garnished with cardamom seeds or dried fruits. It is commonly consumed during festivals such as Holi and Diwali.

Dal Tadka

This classic lentil-based dish originates from the northern parts of India. Although there are variations, the dish is usually prepared with toor dal (split yellow peas), garlic, ginger, onions, tomatoes, garam masala, red chili peppers, ghee, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and fenugreek leaves. Once prepared, dal tadka is usually garnished with coriander leaves and served hot with jeera rice and roti on the side.

Shahi Paneer

Originating from India’s Mughlai cuisine, shahi paneer is a rich, hearty and nutritious cheese curry, prepared with paneer cheese, onions, almond paste, and a rich, spicy tomato-cream sauce. The dish is typically accompanied by Indian breads such as naan, roti, or puri, and garnished with coriander leaves.
